# Vehicle Rental Agreement

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2025

## Agreement Parties

This agreement is between:

- **Dealer/Host** (Vehicle Owner): The individual or entity that owns or has legal authority to rent the vehicle
- **Renter** (Authorized Driver): The individual who is renting the vehicle

**IMPORTANT:** Carsera is not a party to this agreement. Carsera operates solely as a technology platform that facilitates the connection between dealers and renters. Carsera does not own, operate, or control any vehicles listed on the platform.

## 1. Agreement Formation

### 1.1 When Agreement is Created

This rental agreement is formed when:

- A renter creates a booking for a vehicle
- The booking status changes to "confirmed" upon successful payment
- Both parties agree to the terms and conditions set forth herein

### 1.2 Agreement Terms

By completing a booking, both the dealer and renter agree to be bound by:

- This Rental Agreement
- Carsera's Terms of Service
- Any additional terms specified in the vehicle listing
- Applicable local, state, and federal laws

## 2. Vehicle Information and Condition

### 2.1 Vehicle Description

The dealer warrants that:

- All vehicle information provided in the listing is accurate
- The vehicle matches the description (make, model, year, features)
- Any disclosed damage, defects, or limitations are accurately represented
- The vehicle is legally registered and titled

### 2.2 Vehicle Condition Documentation

- Vehicle condition is documented via pre-rental photos
- Both parties should inspect the vehicle at pickup
- Any existing damage should be noted and documented
- Photos serve as evidence of vehicle condition at rental start

### 2.3 Vehicle Availability

The dealer agrees to:

- Make the vehicle available at the agreed pickup location and time
- Ensure the vehicle is in safe, roadworthy condition
- Provide all necessary keys, documentation, and accessories
- Notify the renter immediately of any changes to availability

## 3. Rental Period and Usage

### 3.1 Rental Period

- **Start Date/Time:** As specified in the booking
- **End Date/Time:** As specified in the booking
- **Duration:** The rental period is fixed and cannot be extended without mutual agreement and platform update

### 3.2 Authorized Drivers

- Only the renter and any additional authorized drivers listed in the booking may operate the vehicle
- All drivers must have valid driver's licenses
- All drivers must meet age and license requirements
- Unauthorized drivers are strictly prohibited

### 3.3 Usage Rules

The renter agrees to:

- Use the vehicle only for lawful purposes
- Not engage in any illegal activity while using the vehicle
- Not use the vehicle for commercial purposes (unless explicitly authorized)
- Not sublet or transfer the vehicle to any third party
- Not use the vehicle for racing, towing (unless vehicle is equipped), or off-road use (unless vehicle is designed for such use)
- Comply with all traffic laws and regulations
- Not transport more passengers than the vehicle's seating capacity
- Not transport hazardous materials or illegal substances

### 3.4 Geographic Restrictions

- The vehicle may only be used within the geographic area specified in the listing
- Cross-border travel may be restricted; check with the dealer before booking
- Any geographic restrictions will be clearly stated in the vehicle listing

## 4. Vehicle Return

### 4.1 Return Condition

The renter agrees to return the vehicle:

- At the agreed return location and time
- In the same condition as received, except for normal wear and tear
- With the same fuel level as at pickup (or pay for refueling)
- Clean and free of excessive dirt or debris
- With all original keys, documentation, and accessories

### 4.2 Normal Wear and Tear

Normal wear and tear includes:

- Minor scratches and dings consistent with normal use
- Wear on tires, brakes, and interior from normal operation
- Minor cosmetic wear that does not affect vehicle operation

### 4.3 Damage Assessment

- Any damage beyond normal wear and tear will be assessed
- Damage will be documented with photos
- Repair costs will be determined by the dealer or authorized repair facility
- The renter is responsible for all damage not covered by insurance

## 5. Mileage and Usage Limits

### 5.1 Mileage Limits

- The vehicle listing specifies any mileage limits
- The renter agrees to comply with the stated mileage limit
- Excess mileage charges may apply if limits are exceeded
- Charges for excess mileage will be calculated and charged separately

### 5.2 Mileage Tracking

- Starting mileage is documented at pickup
- Ending mileage is documented at return
- Both parties should verify and agree on mileage readings

## 6. Insurance and Liability

### 6.1 Insurance Requirements

**Dealer Responsibilities:**

- The dealer must maintain valid insurance coverage on the vehicle
- The dealer's insurance must meet state minimum requirements
- The dealer must provide proof of insurance upon request

**Renter Responsibilities:**

- The renter must either:
  - Accept a platform Protection Plan, OR
  - Provide valid personal insurance that meets state minimums
- The renter must provide proof of insurance if using BYOI (Bring Your Own Insurance)
- Insurance coverage must be active for the entire rental period

### 6.2 Damage Responsibility

The renter is responsible for:

- All damages incurred during the rental period
- Damage not covered by the selected insurance or protection plan
- Deductibles as specified in the selected coverage
- Any damage resulting from prohibited use or violation of this agreement

### 6.3 Liability for Accidents

- The renter is responsible for all liability arising from accidents during the rental period
- The renter's insurance (or selected protection plan) is primary coverage
- The dealer's insurance may provide secondary coverage
- Carsera is not liable for any damages, injuries, or losses

## 7. Payment Terms

### 7.1 Rental Fees

- Base rental fee is calculated based on daily rate and rental duration
- All fees are clearly displayed before booking confirmation
- Payment is required in full before vehicle pickup
- Payment is processed through Stripe

### 7.2 Additional Fees

Additional fees may apply for:

- Excess mileage (if applicable)
- Late return
- Cleaning fees (if vehicle is returned excessively dirty)
- Damage repairs (beyond normal wear and tear)
- Fuel charges (if vehicle is not returned with same fuel level)

### 7.3 Platform Fees

- Carsera charges a service fee on each transaction
- Platform fees are clearly disclosed before booking
- Fees are non-refundable except as required by law

## 8. Cancellations and Refunds

### 8.1 Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are subject to the following policy:

- **48+ hours before rental start:** Full refund (including protection plan fees)
- **24-48 hours before rental start:** 50% refund of base rental price, full refund of protection plan fees
- **Less than 24 hours before rental start:** No refund of base rental price, protection plan fees refunded

### 8.2 Cancellation Process

- Cancellations must be made through the Carsera platform
- Refunds will be processed according to the cancellation policy
- Refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear in your account
- Platform fees are non-refundable

### 8.3 Dealer Cancellations

- Dealers may cancel bookings in cases of vehicle unavailability, safety concerns, or other valid reasons
- Renters will receive a full refund if dealer cancels
- Dealers may be subject to penalties for frequent cancellations

## 9. Prohibited Uses

The renter agrees NOT to:

- Use the vehicle for illegal purposes
- Drive under the influence of drugs or alcohol
- Allow unauthorized drivers to operate the vehicle
- Use the vehicle for commercial purposes (unless authorized)
- Sublet or transfer the vehicle
- Use the vehicle for racing, towing (unless equipped), or off-road use (unless designed for such)
- Transport hazardous materials or illegal substances
- Smoke in the vehicle (unless explicitly permitted)
- Transport pets (unless explicitly permitted)
- Remove or disable any safety equipment or tracking devices

## 10. Accidents and Incidents

### 10.1 Accident Reporting

In the event of an accident or incident, the renter must:

- Report the incident to Carsera within 24 hours
- Report the incident to local authorities if required by law
- Not admit fault or liability
- Document the incident with photos and written description
- Provide all requested information and documentation

### 10.2 Claims Process

- Claims are initiated through the Carsera platform
- Carsera facilitates the claims process but does not determine fault
- Insurance companies and/or protection plan administrators handle claims
- The renter is responsible for cooperating with the claims process

## 11. Vehicle Maintenance and Repairs

### 11.1 Routine Maintenance

- The dealer is responsible for routine maintenance and repairs
- The renter should report any mechanical issues immediately
- The renter should not attempt repairs without dealer authorization

### 11.2 Emergency Repairs

- In case of breakdown or mechanical failure, contact the dealer immediately
- Emergency repairs may be authorized by the dealer
- Keep all receipts for reimbursement consideration
- Do not authorize repairs exceeding $100 without dealer approval

## 12. Default and Breach

### 12.1 Renter Default

The renter is in default if they:

- Fail to return the vehicle at the agreed time
- Fail to pay required fees
- Violate any term of this agreement
- Use the vehicle in a prohibited manner
- Cause damage through negligence or prohibited use

### 12.2 Consequences of Default

Upon default, the dealer may:

- Terminate the rental agreement
- Recover the vehicle
- Charge for additional rental days
- Pursue damages and legal remedies
- Report the vehicle as stolen if not returned

## 13. Dispute Resolution

### 13.1 Direct Resolution

- Parties should attempt to resolve disputes directly
- Carsera may facilitate communication but is not a party to disputes
- Carsera does not determine fault or liability

### 13.2 Arbitration

- Disputes that cannot be resolved directly may be subject to binding arbitration
- Arbitration will be conducted in accordance with applicable laws
- The location of arbitration will be in Georgia, United States

## 14. Limitation of Liability

### 14.1 Dealer Liability

The dealer's liability is limited to:

- Vehicle condition and availability as described
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
- Maintaining valid insurance coverage

### 14.2 Renter Liability

The renter's liability includes:

- All damages to the vehicle
- All liability arising from vehicle use
- All costs associated with prohibited use or breach of agreement

### 14.3 Carsera's Role

Carsera is not liable for:

- Vehicle condition, performance, or availability
- Accidents, damages, or injuries
- Disputes between dealers and renters
- Insurance claims or coverage
- Any losses arising from the rental agreement

## 15. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia, United States, without regard to conflict of law principles.

## 16. Entire Agreement

This agreement, together with the booking details and Carsera's Terms of Service, constitutes the entire agreement between the dealer and renter regarding this rental.

## 17. Modifications

This agreement may not be modified except in writing and signed by both parties, or through the Carsera platform with mutual consent.

## 18. Severability

If any provision of this agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

## 19. Acknowledgment

By completing a booking, both parties acknowledge that:

- They have read and understand this agreement
- They agree to be bound by its terms
- They understand that Carsera is not a party to this agreement
- They have had the opportunity to review all terms before booking
